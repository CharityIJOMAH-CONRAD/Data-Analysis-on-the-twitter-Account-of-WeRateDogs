Communicate Insights:¶

The following are my insights from the analysis carried out on the WeRateDogs datasets.

  • Breed of Dog having the highest favorite_count.

  • Breed of Dog having the highest rating_denominator.

  • Breed of Dog having the lowest retweet_count.

In other to plot and actualize our visuals, the package matplotlip.pyploy as plt and %matplotlib inline was imported at the beginning of our wrangling process to handle plots and visuals in the analysis.

Following is the the reading of the saved dataset called twitter_archive_master (the final product of the analysis) in the python workspace using pandas: pd.read_csv.

ploting and visualizing the insights¶

1st Insight: Breed of Dog having the highest favorite_count.¶

A variable called dogs_favorite_count containing the grouping of the breed of dogs was created from the twitter_archive_master dataset using the groupby function and slicing the result to display the first 10 breeds for better visuals.

A bar chat is plotted with 'title = breed_of_dogs_with_favorite_count, ylabel = favorite_count, xlabel = breed_of_dogs and legend' parameters dully defined to properly visualize our insight using the imported plt library.

the visual result: shows that the dog breed: Miniature_Schnauzer has the highest favourite_count in the scale of 10 with over 4000+ favourite_count.
